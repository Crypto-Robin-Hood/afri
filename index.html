    About Afridex

    We invite you to the largest event of its kind, this side of the equator. Join us on a journey of discovery. Contemporary life asks us to understand the global economy. Understanding the origins of the economy will give us insight into its future. The Evolution of Money Learning about the evolution of money is an investment into the future of local and international business, communities and nations. We learn best when learning is an adventure. Travel From barter to Crypto and post to Blockchain, be thrilled, inspired and informed.
